Why We Built This
TPIL runs 150+ programs/year across 5 types serving thousands of engineers across APAC. Program planning was tracked in spreadsheets (40+ tasks each), banners required design requests with multi-day turnaround, reporting meant manual copy-paste from L++ exports, and leadership had no single dashboard for program health. Data lived in silos across UDMS, L++, Google Sheets, and Slack. This hub consolidates everything into one self-service platform.
What It Does
Dashboard
Real-time stats from program data. Attendance, CSAT, trainer feedback. Color-coded by program type. AI-powered Salesforce news feed.
Banner Creator
8 templates, 6 sizes. AI-generated titles, Slack posts & background images via Gemini. Download as PNG.
Program Planner
Auto-generates 40-task checklist across 6 phases from launch date. Inline editing, CSV export.
Reports & Programs
Upload L++ data for instant CSAT visualization. 5 dedicated program pages with filtered stats & detail tables.
